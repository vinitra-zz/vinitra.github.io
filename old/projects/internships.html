LinkedIn

During the summer of 2015, I worked at LinkedIn Headquarters in Mountain View as a Software Engineering Intern. As part of the Search Engine Optimization team in the Growth division, my main project was fullstack, involving UI testing frameworks. My other projects involved distributed computing (Hadoop) and URL generation. I also got the wonderful chance to present at All Hands with CEO Jeff Weiner and work with the User Experience Design team on a market research experiment.

Google

Prior to that, I spent 3 years developing curriculum for Google's Made With Code Initiative and was an ambassador to their events -- introducing coding to over 15,000 girls in California. I spent the summer of 2011 as a Google CAPE (Computing and Programming Experience) Intern, learning what it meant to be a Computer Scientist and shadowing different types of Google engineers. I was later recognized as one of Google's 30 International Trailblazers in Computer Science, working with talented individuals around the globe on projects to bridge the digital divide.

IBM Research

I will be working on Machine Learning and Analytics for Cloud Computing with IBM Research in New York this summer!
